06

Howdy, I'm Brennan.

I started in construction, earning a Bachelor of Science in Construction Science, because I wanted to understand how projects get built before I ever tried to finance them. That foundation is the through-line of everything I have thoughtfully pursued: a dual MBA in finance and Master of Land & Property Development at Texas A&M, aimed at the place where capital strategy and project execution meet.

I am comfortable building pro formas, structuring capital stacks, and working through cash-flow projections, but I also know where projects hit friction in the field, the schedule, entitlement, or the budget. That mix let me work as an owner's representative at Howard Hughes on a Ritz-Carlton condominium, coordinate scopes on a 58-story Austin high-rise at Ryan Companies, and help deliver a mass-timber office building.

I am proudest of leading the finance work for an $828M mixed-use plan in the ULI Hines Competition alongside a team of architects. This past July I joined KKR's K-Star team in Dallas as a Direct Lending Analyst.

Off the clock I am usually training for a Hyrox competition with my fiancée, outside of the city lines, or hiking. And I am an Aggie through and through.
